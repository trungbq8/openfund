Getting Started

How do I create an account?

To create a Raiser account on OpenFund:

  1. Click on "Log In/Sign Up" in the top navigation bar
  2. Fill out the registration form with your personal information
  3. Connect your crypto wallet to verify ownership
  4. Complete the email verification process

For investors, you only need to connect your wallet by clicking the wallet icon in the navigation bar.

Which wallets are supported?

OpenFund currently supports the following wallets:

  • MetaMask
  • Binance Web3
  • OKX Web3
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet

Make sure your wallet is configured to connect to the Sei Network.

For Project Creators (Raisers)

How do I submit a project for fundraising?

To submit a project for fundraising:

  1. Log in to your Raiser account
  2. Click on "New Project" from your dashboard
  3. Fill out the project details form, including:
    • Project name and description
    • Token information (name, symbol, supply, etc.)
    • Funding goal and timeline
    • Project links (website, whitepaper, social media)
  4. Submit your project for review

Our team will review your submission and may contact you for additional information.

What are the fees for project creators?

OpenFund charges a 5% fee on the total amount raised by successful projects. This fee is automatically deducted when funds are released to project creators.

Additional blockchain transaction fees may apply, which are set by the network and not by OpenFund.

For Investors

How do I invest in a project?

To invest in a project:

  1. Connect your wallet by clicking the wallet icon in the navigation
  2. Browse available projects on the "Invest" page
  3. Click "View more" on a project that interests you
  4. Enter the amount you wish to invest
  5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet

Upon successful completion, your investment will be recorded on the blockchain, and you'll receive tokens according to the project's terms.

What happens if a project fails?

After the funding period ends, there are 3 days investors voting on fund distribution. If more than 50% investment amount request a refund, the project is marked as 'Funding Failed' and in 1 day after that, any token holder seeking a refund can reclaim 100% of their invested funds by returning their tokens to the contract. Otherwise, the full amount is released to the fundraiser.

This mechanism reduces the risk of funding projects that fail to develop.

Technical Support

My transaction failed. What should I do?

If your transaction failed, try these steps:

  1. Check if you have enough funds to cover the transaction amount plus gas fees
  2. Verify that you're connected to the correct network (Sei Network)
  3. Try refreshing the page and attempting the transaction again
  4. Clear your browser cache and reconnect your wallet

If the issue persists, please contact our support team with details about the failed transaction, including any error messages and your wallet address.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team through any of the following channels:

  • Email: support@openfund.live
  • Telegram: @OpenFund_Support
  • Discord: OpenFund Community (Support channel)

Our support team is available 24/7 and typically responds within 24 hours.
